About Me

My name is Nicolas, I am 21 years old, a naturalized Brazilian, and fluent in English. I have been working with programming for six years. In 2020, I began a technical high school program at PUCPR (TECPUC), where my interest in technology and software development grew even stronger.

Since studying Architecture and Urbanism, I have been driven by the idea of making the world a slightly better place by building solutions that genuinely help people. Although the course itself did not align with my long-term goals, this mindset stayed with me.

Projects

CWB+

CWB+ is a public safety application that allows users to view, report, and track risk-related incidents in Curitiba, such as robberies, thefts, and traffic accidents.

The platform leverages geolocation to display high-risk areas on an interactive map, providing real-time alerts about potential dangers near the user's location. In addition, the system enables anonymous reporting with image uploads, community-based incident validation, and a personal history of submitted reports.

APIs: Leaflet.js

Motivation: The project emerged from the need to democratize access to public safety information and empower people to make safer decisions in unfamiliar areas of Curitiba.
